Welcome to ATAL Tinkering Lab - APS2 Jabalpur

The ATAL Tinkering Lab at Army Public School No. 2, Jabalpur is a state-of-the-art facility that encourages innovation, creativity, and hands-on learning. Students get access to tools and equipment to explore STEM concepts, work on projects, and develop real-world problem-solving skills.

Our mission is to foster curiosity, creativity, and imagination in young minds and to inculcate skills such as design mindset, computational thinking, adaptive learning, and physical computing.
